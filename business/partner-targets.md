# Partner Targets
## Introduction
To further enhance the capabilities of Thought-Bridge, we have identified key partners to integrate with, focusing on SaaS and APIs that complement our product's mission to provide accurate and reliable high-level thinking and low-level implementation for AI-powered tools.

## Partner Integration Roadmap
The following partners have been selected based on their potential to add significant value to Thought-Bridge users, with a focus on those offering affiliate or revenue-share programs.

1. **GitHub**
   - **Free-tier limits:** Unlimited public repositories, 500 MB of storage, and 100,000 GitHub Actions minutes.
   - **Integration effort:** Medium (M)
   - **Value-add:** Enhances version control and collaboration for Thought-Bridge users, solving the job of managing and tracking changes in AI-powered tool development.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-share:** GitHub offers a developer program but no direct affiliate program for integrations. However, integrating with GitHub can open opportunities for revenue share through the GitHub Marketplace.

2. **AWS SageMaker**
   - **Free-tier limits:** 12 months free for new AWS customers, with limited usage hours and storage.
   - **Integration effort:** Large (L)
   - **Value-add:** Provides a comprehensive platform for building, training, and deploying machine learning models, solving the job of scalable model deployment for Thought-Bridge users.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-share:** AWS offers a revenue-share model through its AWS Partner Network (APN).

3. **Google Cloud AI Platform**
   - **Free-tier limits:** $300 free credit for new customers, usable over 90 days.
   - **Integration effort:** Large (L)
   - **Value-add:** Offers managed platform services for building, deploying, and managing machine learning models, solving the scalability and reliability needs of Thought-Bridge users.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-share:** Google Cloud provides a partner advantage program with potential for revenue share.

4. **Hugging Face Transformers**
   - **Free-tier limits:** Unlimited model usage with limited compute resources.
   - **Integration effort:** Small (S)
   - **Value-add:** Provides pre-trained models and a simple interface for using transformer models, solving the job of accessing high-quality, pre-trained AI models for Thought-Bridge users.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-share:** Hugging Face offers a model hub with potential for revenue share through model usage fees.

5. **Notion**
   - **Free-tier limits:** Unlimited blocks and pages, with file upload limits.
   - **Integration effort:** Medium (M)
   - **Value-add:** Enhances documentation, knowledge management, and collaboration for Thought-Bridge users, solving the job of organizing and sharing project knowledge.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-share:** Notion offers an affiliate program for referrals.

6. **Zapier**
   - **Free-tier limits:** 100 tasks per month, with limited automation capabilities.
   - **Integration effort:** Small (S)
   - **Value-add:** Automates workflows across multiple web applications, solving the job of integrating Thought-Bridge with other tools and services used by developers.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-share:** Zapier offers an affiliate program with revenue share opportunities.

7. **CircleCI**
   - **Free-tier limits:** Unlimited users, with limits on compute resources and concurrency.
   - **Integration effort:** Medium (M)
   - **Value-add:** Provides continuous integration and continuous deployment (CI/CD) pipelines, solving the job of automating testing and deployment for Thought-Bridge users.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-share:** CircleCI offers a partner program with potential for revenue share.

8. **Datadog**
   - **Free-tier limits:** 1 host, with limited data retention and metrics.
   - **Integration effort:** Medium (M)
   - **Value-add:** Offers monitoring and analytics for cloud-scale applications, solving the job of performance monitoring and optimization for Thought-Bridge users.
   - **Affiliate/Revenue-share:** Datadog provides a partner network with opportunities for revenue share.

## Prioritization
Prioritization of these integrations will be based on the value-add to Thought-Bridge users, the potential for revenue share or affiliate income, and the integration effort required. Initial focus will be on Hugging Face Transformers and GitHub due to their low integration effort and high value-add potential.
