# Breakeven Analysis

## Cost per Active User
- **Compute**: $0.05/user/mo (assuming 1000 requests/user/mo at $0.00005/request)
- **Storage**: $0.01/user/mo (assuming 1GB storage/user at $0.01/GB/mo)
- **Bandwidth**: $0.02/user/mo (assuming 10GB bandwidth/user/mo at $0.002/GB)
- **Total Cost per Active User**: $0.08/user/mo

## Pricing Tiers
1. **Basic**: $20/mo
   - Features: Basic high-level thinking, limited low-level implementation
2. **Pro**: $50/mo
   - Features: Advanced high-level thinking, standard low-level implementation
3. **Enterprise**: $150/mo
   - Features: Premium high-level thinking, comprehensive low-level implementation, priority support

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Range
- **Low End**: $50/user (organic marketing, referrals)
- **High End**: $200/user (paid advertising, targeted campaigns)
- **Average CAC**: $125/user

## Lifetime Value (LTV) Estimate
- **Average Subscription Length**: 12 months
- **Average Revenue per User (ARPU)**:
  - Basic: $20/mo × 12 = $240
  - Pro: $50/mo × 12 = $600
  - Enterprise: $150/mo × 12 = $1800
- **Average LTV**: $940/user (weighted average based on expected distribution of tiers)

## Break-even Users Count
- **Total Cost to Break Even**: CAC + Monthly Costs
  - CAC: $125/user
  - Monthly Costs: $0.08/user/mo × 12 = $0.96/user
  - Total: $125.96/user
- **Break-even Users Count**: $125.96 / ($50 average price tier) = ~3 users

## Path to $10K MRR
- **Basic Tier**: 500 users × $20/mo = $10,000 MRR
- **Pro Tier**: 200 users × $50/mo = $10,000 MRR
- **Enterprise Tier**: 67 users × $150/mo = $10,000 MRR
