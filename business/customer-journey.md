# Customer Journey Map for Thought-Bridge
## Aware Phase
### Trigger Event
- **Event**: Developer or AI/ML engineer stumbles upon a problem that requires high-level thinking and low-level implementation for AI-powered tools.
- **Source**: Online search, social media, or word of mouth.

### Friction Points
- **Lack of awareness**: Not knowing that such a tool exists.
- **Difficulty in finding relevant solutions**: Existing tools either focus on high-level thinking or low-level implementation, but not both.

### User Emotions
- **Frustration**: Struggling to find a solution that meets their needs.
- **Curiosity**: Wanting to explore new tools and technologies.

### Opportunities to Delight
- **Relevant content**: Blog posts, videos, or podcasts that highlight the importance of balancing high-level thinking and low-level implementation.
- **Personalized recommendations**: AI-powered suggestions for tools and resources based on their specific needs and interests.

### Metric per Phase
- **Awareness**: 10% of target audience is aware of Thought-Bridge (metric: website traffic, social media engagement).

## Consider Phase
### Trigger Event
- **Event**: Developer or AI/ML engineer discovers Thought-Bridge and is interested in learning more.
- **Source**: Online search, social media, or word of mouth.

### Friction Points
- **Lack of trust**: Uncertainty about the tool's effectiveness and reliability.
- **High expectations**: Believing that the tool should be perfect and meet all their needs.

### User Emotions
- **Hope**: Wanting to find a solution that meets their needs.
- **Apprehension**: Worried about the tool's limitations and potential drawbacks.

### Opportunities to Delight
- **Case studies**: Real-world examples of Thought-Bridge's success stories and testimonials.
- **Free trials**: Offering a free trial or demo to let users experience the tool firsthand.

### Metric per Phase
- **Consideration**: 20% of aware users consider Thought-Bridge (metric: free trial sign-ups, demo requests).

## Try Phase
### Trigger Event
- **Event**: Developer or AI/ML engineer decides to try Thought-Bridge.
- **Source**: Free trial, demo, or recommendation from a trusted source.

### Friction Points
- **Steep learning curve**: Difficulty in understanding how to use the tool effectively.
- **Limited resources**: Insufficient documentation, support, or community resources.

### User Emotions
- **Excitement**: Eager to try out the tool and see its potential.
- **Frustration**: Struggling to overcome the learning curve and resource limitations.

### Opportunities to Delight
- **Easy onboarding**: Streamlined and intuitive onboarding process that gets users started quickly.
- **Community support**: Active and responsive community that provides help and guidance.

### Metric per Phase
- **Try**: 30% of considered users try Thought-Bridge (metric: free trial completion rate, demo engagement).

## Adopt Phase
### Trigger Event
- **Event**: Developer or AI/ML engineer decides to adopt Thought-Bridge as a regular tool.
- **Source**: Positive experience with the tool, word of mouth, or recommendation from a trusted source.

### Friction Points
- **Cost**: Concerns about the tool's cost and whether it fits within their budget.
- **Integration**: Difficulty in integrating the tool with their existing workflow and systems.

### User Emotions
- **Confidence**: Trusting in the tool's effectiveness and reliability.
- **Satisfaction**: Happy with the tool's performance and value.

### Opportunities to Delight
- **Competitive pricing**: Affordable pricing that meets their budget needs.
- **Seamless integration**: Easy integration with their existing workflow and systems.

### Metric per Phase
- **Adoption**: 50% of tried users adopt Thought-Bridge (metric: paid subscription rate, regular usage).

## Expand Phase
### Trigger Event
- **Event**: Developer or AI/ML engineer decides to expand their use of Thought-Bridge to other areas of their work.
- **Source**: Positive experience with the tool, word of mouth, or recommendation from a trusted source.

### Friction Points
- **Limited features**: Believing that the tool lacks features or functionality that they need.
- **Scalability**: Difficulty in scaling the tool to meet their growing needs.

### User Emotions
- **Excitement**: Eager to explore new features and capabilities.
- **Confidence**: Trusting in the tool's ability to meet their growing needs.

### Opportunities to Delight
- **Regular updates**: Frequent updates with new features and improvements.
- **Enterprise support**: Dedicated support for large-scale deployments and complex needs.

### Metric per Phase
- **Expansion**: 70% of adopted users expand their use of Thought-Bridge (metric: feature adoption rate, usage growth).
